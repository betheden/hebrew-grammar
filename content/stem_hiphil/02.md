# stem_hiphil Article
The hiphil / הפעיל [stem](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/stem/02.md) is characterized by a preformative ה before the root letters of the [verb](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/verb/02.md) in the [perfect](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/verb_perfect/02.md) form. This ה changes in the [participle](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/participle_active/02.md) and [imperfect](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/verb_imperfect/02.md) forms (see table). The meaning is typically causative or idiomatic of action. In many cases the [noun](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/noun/02.md) derived from the same root is the object or result of the hiphil verb associated with that root, for example:

מָטָ֔ר = rain 

הִמְטִ֜יר = cause/make rain down
