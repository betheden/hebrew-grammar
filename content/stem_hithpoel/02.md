# stem_hithpoel Article
The Hithpoel stem is a variation of the [Hithpael stem](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/stem_hithpael/02.md) and expresses the reflexive sense of the verbal action of the [Poel stem](https://git.door43.org/Door43/en-uhg/src/master/content/stem_poel/02.md). The Hithpoel stem is formed from the Hithpael stem by a vowel shift. It is an extremely rare verbal from in Biblical Hebrew.  It is recommended to *always* consult a dictionary or lexicon for the precise nuance of any verb in the Hithpoel stem.
