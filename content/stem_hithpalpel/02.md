# stem_hithpalpel Article
Hithpalpel has similar meanings to hithpael. Here are examples of forms:

* DAN 11:11

    * יִתְמַרְמַר

    * will become very angry 

    * יִתְמַרְמַר comes from the root מרר

* PSA 119:16

    * אֶשְׁתַּעֲשָׁ֑ע 

    * I will delight
    
    * אֶשְׁתַּעֲשָׁ֑ע comes from the root שׁעע
