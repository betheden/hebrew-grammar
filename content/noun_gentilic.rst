Noun Gentilic
=============

Summary
-------

A gentilic noun is a noun that describes the place of the noun, such as
its nationality.

Article
-------

Hebrew technically does not have gentilic nouns, but sometimes a
:ref:`adjective_gentilic`
is parsed as a
:ref:`adjective_gentilic`.
